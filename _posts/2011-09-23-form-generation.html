  1. Two parameters
  2. Six global variables
  3. Three switch statements (containing 30 total cases)
  4. 17 if() statements
  5. Nine while() statements
  6. (At least) 13 queries
  7. Four eval() statements
  8. Countless unquoted array keys
  9. Stupid deep control structure nesting
There's more, but I can't bear to keep digging through the code.
