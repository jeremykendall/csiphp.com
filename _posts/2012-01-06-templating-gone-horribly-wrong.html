Imagine the confusion the below code causes when, as you're reviewing some new code, you run across a seemingly random </head> tag. Where is the rest of the markup, you might ask? Why, take a look at doPageHead(), silly. 
function doPageHead($lang='en') {

  print "
    
    
      
  ";
}
I'm not a big fan of functions that simply echo HTML, but if you're going to do something like this, at least include the closing head tag. Seriously. Or I might come find you and punch you square in the jeans. Another, better option is to find a PHP templating engine that'll do most of your templating for you.
